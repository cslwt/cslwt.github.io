Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Jianke Zhu and Prof. Lei Zhang(PolyU, HK). My research interests are in the field of computer vision, especially on the techniques for object detection, instance segmentation and panoptic segmentaion under minimal human supervision or without human annotations.
