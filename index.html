About me

I am a Ph.D. student at College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, fortunately supervised by Prof. Jianke Zhu and Prof. Lei Zhang (PolyU, HK). My recent research interests are multimodal large language models, vision-language models and open-world segmentation. Before, I mainly focus on the field of the techniques for object detection, image segmentaion under minimal human supervision, including label-efficient /weakly-supervised /un-supervised approaches. Besides, I am also interested in autonomous driving tasks (HD-Map, 3D-Occupancy, etc.) and 3D reconstruction tasks.
